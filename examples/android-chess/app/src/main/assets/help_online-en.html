Play online Help

FICS: Free Internet Chess Server lets you play with thousands of chess players online.
Warning: if your device switches from one internet access point to another, the connection can break. There is no timeseal.

Menu

Challenges
This shows players seeking a match. Click on the player in the list to accept a challenge.
Seek
Set up your own challenge. If you fill in the handle of a player you know, it will make a direct challenge.
Games
Watch games being played online. A grandmaster can be identified by the GM prefix to the handle.
Players
Shows idle players with ratings.

Buttons

The two on-screen buttons can be used to:
- access a list of useful functions
- whisper button - allows registered users to chat with other observers of the game.
See the observers of the game by typing: allobservers [game number]

Examine

Go over the game with different lines. Use revert button to get back to main line.
Have a coach follow you. Allow a player to make moves by typing: mexamine [player]
Use an internet phone to have voice chat. (eg. Skype)
When finished - type: unexamine
