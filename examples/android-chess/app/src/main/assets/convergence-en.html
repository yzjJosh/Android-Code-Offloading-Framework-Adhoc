Chess TV Screen - experimental

This gives you the ability to let another device show the chess-board when on the same Local Area Network.

Currently, this is experimental and the actual Smart-TV application that can connect to your Android device is not yet available.

Let me know if you want to beta test this (if you have a Smart TV)

Step 1. Press the "Start" button from the "Share screen" window on your Android device

Step 2. Start the "Chess screen" application on your TV

Step 3. Enter the number that is shown below the "Start" button using the remote from your TV.
